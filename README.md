# CHARLES SANDERS PEIRCE · XIII

> A sphere of **UD0** — the ROOT0 universe / biosphere. Domain: **LOGIKĒ**.

CHARLES SANDERS PEIRCE (1839–1914) — LOGIKĒ XIII, the great American original, by many lights the most powerful logician between Aristotle and Gödel, robbed of credit by his own neglect. TWO of his firsts sit at this lineage's heart. ⚑ FUNCTIONAL COMPLETENESS: a SINGLE connective suffices for all of logic — his 'ampheck', the arrow ↓ (today's NOR) — stated 1880, THREE DECADES before Sheffer's stroke; the deep reason a chip is one gate tiled billions of times. ⚑ RELATIONS & QUANTIFIERS: independently of Frege (1883–85) he built predicate logic and coined the notation the world still uses — Σ for 'there exists' (a logical sum), Π for 'for all' (a logical product). LIVE: (1) NOR-universality — toggle A/B, watch NOT/AND/OR built from NOTHING BUT the arrow ↓, each reproducing its standard truth table (verified 10 / 0001 / 0111); (2) Σ/Π relations — a clickable R(x,y) matrix where Σx Πy ('one x relates to every y') ≠ Πy Σx ('each y has some x'), the quantifier-order distinction at the root of all mathematics (verified). CONFIDENCE-TIERED: STRONG (NOR-completeness, relations, the Σ/Π notation — rigorous, foundational, genuinely his and often EARLIEST) / ECLIPSED (published little & obscurely, much left in unread manuscripts; Frege/Russell/Sheffer got the glory for things he'd done — largely self-inflicted) / SPRAWLING (⚠ a whole cosmos beyond logic — pragmatism, semiotics, metaphysics, geodesy — much unfinished & contested). The man: lost his one professorship (Johns Hopkins) to a chaotic personal life, fell after 1891 into deepening poverty, spent his last years cold & hungry in rural Pennsylvania writing thousands of unread pages, kept alive by quiet help from William James — the arrow that builds every gate and the Σ/Π in every textbook recognised only long after he was gone. ⚑ Belongs beside [[george-boole]] & [[gottlob-frege]] in the spine; the lineage met him late, as the world did.
